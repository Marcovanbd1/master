The Governance Protocol: Trust and Technical Architecture

This document outlines the ethical and technical framework that underpins Support Playlists, ensuring safety, privacy, and actionable insight at scale.

1. The Zero-Data Architecture (The Trust Protocol)

Our core commitment is to remove all data liability for our partners (NHS, Enterprise) and all privacy risk for the citizen.

  • **No Logins/Accounts:** The user is anonymous by design.
  • **Ephemeral Input:** All natural language input is processed instantly and deleted immediately after the Support Playlist is generated. Nothing is stored.
  • **No Tracking:** We operate without cookies, session tracking, or IP logging (validated via Plausible Analytics integration).
  • **GDPR Shield:** By eliminating Personably Identifiable Information (PII) collection, we eliminate GDPR compliance risk related to user data.

2. ANCI: Autonomous Non-Clinical Intelligence

ANCI is the proprietary language model that translates unstructured emotional text into structured, actionable support steps.

  • **Non-Diagnostic:** The model is trained *only* to identify needs (housing, debt, social support, self-care) and never to diagnose clinical conditions.
  • **Clinical Safety:** This non-diagnostic focus de-risks deployment. We operate entirely outside the clinical safety burden of AI as a Medical Device (AIaMD).
  • **Peer-Validation:** ANCI’s output is trained and constantly refined against a library of peer-validated support pathways.

3. DSI: Demand Shaping Intelligence

DSI is the aggregated, anonymized data stream that provides system-level insight. This is the only information our infrastructure retains.

  • **Anonymous Aggregation:** We aggregate sentiment (e.g., "high volume of anxiety mentioning housing debt") and time stamps. No user inputs or identities are retained.
  • **Civic Radar:** This data acts as an early warning system, allowing ICS leaders to commission resources (VCSE funding, social prescribing services) proactively, based on real-time population need.
  • **Ethical Filtering:** DSI is filtered to ensure no micro-segments could potentially lead to de-anonymization.

4. Clinical and Ethical Oversight

To maintain trust and technical rigor, our platform is subject to continuous review.

  • **Clinical Red Team:** External clinical advisors constantly test the model output to ensure safety and prevent therapeutic drift.
  • **Regulatory Alignment:** Deployment follows the highest standards of the NHS Code of Conduct for Data and the principles of DCB0129 (Clinical Risk Management Standard).
