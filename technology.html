The Governance Protocol

This document outlines the ethical and technical framework that underpins Mood, ensuring safety, privacy, and actionable insight at scale.

1. Zero-Data Architecture

Our core commitment is to remove all data liability for our Integration Partners (Rapid Health, NHS, Enterprise) and all privacy risk for the user.

No Logins/Accounts: The user is anonymous by design.

Ephemeral Input: All natural language input is processed instantly and deleted immediately after the Playlist is generated. Nothing is stored.

No Tracking: We operate without cookies, session tracking, or IP logging.

GDPR Shield: By eliminating Personally Identifiable Information (PII) collection, we remove the primary compliance risks associated with user data storage.

2. ANCI: Autonomous Non-Clinical Intelligence

ANCI is the proprietary logic engine that translates unstructured emotional text into structured, actionable navigational steps.

Navigation, Not Diagnosis: The model is trained only to identify needs (housing, debt, social support, self-care) and never to diagnose clinical conditions.

Operational Safety: This focus de-risks deployment. We operate strictly as an intelligent routing layer, removing the regulatory burden associated with AI as a Medical Device (AIaMD).

Peer-Validation: ANCI’s routing logic is trained and refined against a library of peer-validated support pathways.

3. DSI: Demand Shaping Intelligence

DSI is the aggregated, anonymized data stream that provides system-level insight. This is the only information our infrastructure retains.

Anonymous Aggregation: We aggregate sentiment (e.g., "high volume of anxiety mentioning housing debt") and time stamps. No user inputs or identities are retained.

Civic Radar: This data acts as an early warning system, allowing leaders to commission resources proactively based on real-time population stress signals.

4. Oversight & Scope

To maintain trust and technical rigor, our platform is subject to continuous review.

Safety Review: External advisors constantly test the model output to ensure safe routing and prevent navigational drift.

Regulatory Alignment: Mood is an intelligent navigation utility, not a medical device. We do not diagnose, treat, or prescribe. We align with the highest standards of Data Ethics and Safe Routing to ensure that non-clinical demand is resolved effectively while clinical needs are clearly signposted.
